The research
5 min
The window where most missed callers can still be won back. After that, they have already booked elsewhere.

Speed of response is the single biggest factor in whether a caller becomes a booking.

Multiple studies of inbound phone and web inquiries (Harvard Business Review, MIT, and the Lead Response Management Study among them) have shown a sharp drop in conversion when callers are not reached quickly. Plain English: the faster you pick up, the more likely they book. Wait an hour and most of them are already eating somewhere else.

For a restaurant, "speed of response" really means one thing: did the phone get answered. Voicemail is not a response. A callback the next morning is not a response.
