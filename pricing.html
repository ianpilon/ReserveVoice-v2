Pricing questions

Common questions about billing and what's included.

What does $149 a month include?

Unlimited calls answered 24/7. Live reservation booking into OpenTable, Resy, SevenRooms, Tock, or Google Calendar. Automatic SMS confirmations and reminders. Multilingual support. Full call transcripts and dashboard. Customer success support. No usage limits, no per-call charges, no overage surprises.

Is there a setup fee?

No. Setup is on us. We configure your assistant, connect it to your reservation system, train the voice on your menu and policies, and run test calls with you before going live. Most restaurants are fully set up within 48 hours.

Is there a contract or commitment?

Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. No annual commitment, no early-termination fees. If it is not working for your restaurant, you stop paying.

How does flat-rate pricing actually work?

One monthly fee. Unlimited calls. No per-minute billing, no overage charges, no surprise invoices on a busy Saturday. Your cost is the same whether you get 50 calls a week or 500.

What if my restaurant gets a much higher call volume than typical?

$149 covers normal restaurant call volume, roughly a few hundred calls per month. If you run a very high-volume operation that consistently exceeds that, we will reach out and have a conversation about a tier that makes sense for both sides. We do not surprise-bill you.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Billed monthly on the day you sign up. Annual billing available on request if you would prefer that.

Where does the $87K figure come from?

An average independent restaurant: party of 3, $35 per person, 8 missed or unreturned calls per week, plus reduced no-shows from SMS reminders and a small share of repeat-guest lifetime value. Your numbers will be different. We will model your restaurant specifically on the meeting.
